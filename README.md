nox-core is a browser designed for privacy. It keeps your internet activity separate for every account you own. Use it to log into multiple websites at once without the sites tracking your real identity. This software stores all your data on your computer. It sends no tracking information to any server.

## ⚙️ Setting up the software

Find the file you saved. Double-click the file to start the installation. Windows may show a security prompt because the app is new. Click More Info and then click Run anyway to continue. Follow the simple steps on your screen. The installer places a shortcut on your desktop.

## 🌐 Understanding your profiles

A profile functions like a separate browser window. Each profile keeps its own history, cookies, and login details. Websites see every profile as a unique visitor. This prevents sites from linking your accounts. Create as many profiles as you need for different tasks, such as work, shopping, or personal use.

## 🛡️ Protecting your identity

nox-core manages your browser fingerprint. Fingerprints are bits of information that websites use to track you, like your screen size or your operating system version. This browser masks that information. It presents a standard, common fingerprint to every website you visit. This makes your browser look like thousands of other common browsers, which helps you blend in.

## ⚡ Using the proxy manager

Add your own proxy settings to each profile. A proxy acts as a middleman between your computer and the internet. It changes the location websites see for your connection. Use SOCKS5 or HTTP proxies to change your digital location. Navigate to the proxy tab inside your profile settings, click Add Proxy, and paste your proxy details. The browser saves these details for that specific profile only.

## 📁 Storing your data locally

This software works 100% on your local machine. No other computer or cloud server stores your data or your browsing history. When you close the browser, your information stays exactly where you left it on your hard drive. Back up your data by copying the installation folder to an external drive or a secure cloud service.

## 📝 Tips for success

- Keep your software updated. Check the releases page once a month for new features and better privacy tools.
- Use one profile for one specific service. Do not mix your personal logins with business logins in the same profile.
- Clear your cache if a website acts strange. You can find this option in the profile tools menu.
- Use strong, unique passwords for every account you manage in the browser.
- Close the browser when you leave your desk to keep your profiles secure.

## 💻 System requirements for Windows

nox-core requires a modern version of Windows. Ensure you use Windows 10 or Windows 11 for the best experience. The application runs best with at least 4GB of memory. Use a solid-state drive to ensure fast startup times for your browser profiles. Make sure your internet connection stays stable while you perform tasks that require multiple accounts.

## 🔍 Troubleshooting common issues

If the browser does not open, restart your computer. Sometimes antivirus programs block new software. Check your antivirus settings to ensure nox-core has permission to run. If a webpage does not load, verify your proxy settings. Copy the proxy address carefully to avoid typos. If your profiles take too long to load, clear your browser history within the profile settings to save space.

## 🛠️ Advanced proxy features

You can assign a different proxy to every profile. This creates a unique digital footprint for every account. If you manage social media accounts, assign a unique static proxy to each account. This prevents the platform from flagging your accounts for suspicious login locations. The browser checks your proxy connection before you go to any website. If the proxy fails, it stops the connection to protect your real identity.
